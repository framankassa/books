About This Book

Set during Mussolini's 1935 invasion of Ethiopia, "The Shadow King" is a gorgeously crafted and unputdownable exploration of female power and what it means to be a woman at war. The novel tells the story of Hirut, a servant girl who becomes a warrior when Ethiopia is invaded by Italy.

Maaza Mengiste shines a light on the women soldiers written out of African and European history. This is a breathtaking exploration of what it means to be a woman at war, as well as an emotionally gripping story about the strength of the human spirit.
