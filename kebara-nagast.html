About This Book

The "Kebra Nagast" (Glory of Kings) is Ethiopia's greatest national epic and a sacred text that recounts the relationship between King Solomon and the Queen of Sheba, and how their son Menelik brought the Ark of the Covenant to Ethiopia. This foundational text has shaped Ethiopian identity and religious thought for centuries.

This edition presents the complete text in an accessible English translation, with extensive notes and commentary. The translation preserves the majesty and spiritual power of the original while making this important work available to modern readers.
