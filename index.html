About EthioReads

EthioReads is Ethiopia's premier online bookstore, founded in 2015 with a mission to promote Ethiopian literature and make it accessible to readers worldwide. We specialize in curating the finest collection of books by Ethiopian authors, both in print and digital formats.

Our team of passionate book lovers carefully selects each title in our collection, ensuring we offer works that represent the rich diversity of Ethiopian culture, history, and storytelling traditions. From classic Amharic literature to contemporary English works by Ethiopian authors, we celebrate the full spectrum of our literary heritage.

Beyond just selling books, we're committed to fostering a vibrant reading culture in Ethiopia. We host author events, book clubs, and literary festivals to connect readers with writers and create a community around Ethiopian literature.

Our Mission

To preserve and promote Ethiopian literary heritage while supporting contemporary authors and making their works accessible to readers worldwide.

Our Vision

To become the global platform for Ethiopian literature, connecting readers with the rich stories and ideas emerging from Ethiopia.

Our Values

Quality, authenticity, cultural preservation, and community building through the power of literature.
