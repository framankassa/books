About This Book

"Dertogada" (·ãµ·à≠·âÜ·ã≥) is a groundbreaking novel by Yismake Worku that masterfully blends Amharic literary tradition with elements of American spy thrillers and conspiracy novels. This postcolonial critique tells the gripping story of an international spy agency's attempt to capture an Ethiopian-born rocket scientist.

The narrative unfolds through sophisticated layers, exploring multiple narrative levels and the mechanisms used by the narrator to create intersecting storylines. With elements of gender switching, disguise, and role reversal, the novel centers around a mysterious word tattooed on the backs of the main characters - a symbol that drives the plot's tension and mystery.
